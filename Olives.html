Olives

Welcome to Olives Restaurant, where the sun-kissed flavors of the Mediterranean dance on your palate. Our carefully curated menu brings together a delightful selection of dishes inspired by the diverse cuisines of the region. Each item is thoughtfully prepared using the freshest ingredients to ensure a truly authentic and unforgettable dining experience.

Starters:

1. Greek Mezze Platter: Embark on a culinary journey with a selection of traditional Greek mezze, including creamy hummus, tangy tzatziki, stuffed grape leaves, and more.
2. Spanish Patatas Bravas: Crispy potato cubes served with a smoky and spicy tomato sauce, a staple of Spanish tapas.
3. Italian Caprese Salad: Taste the simplicity of Italy with ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, basil leaves, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze.
