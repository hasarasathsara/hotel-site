Olives Restaurant:
Indulge in a sensory journey at Olives, where the flavors of the Mediterranean come to life. Our restaurant offers a tantalizing array of dishes inspired by the rich culinary traditions of the region. From mouthwatering Greek moussaka to savory Spanish paella, each dish is carefully crafted using the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes. Experience the warmth of Mediterranean hospitality in a relaxed and inviting ambiance, making every meal at Olives a truly unforgettable dining experience.

Infinity Pool Bar:
Elevate your poolside experience at our Infinity Pool Bar, where luxury meets leisure. Perched on the edge of an endless horizon, this chic bar offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape, creating an oasis of serenity. Sip on expertly crafted cocktails, mocktails, and a curated selection of beverages as you bask in the sun or unwind by the pool. With a range of light bites and snacks to complement your drink of choice, the Infinity Pool Bar promises to be the ultimate destination for relaxation and refreshment.

Sky Lounge:
Reach for the stars at our exclusive Sky Lounge, an elevated escape above the city's hustle and bustle. Nestled high above the skyline, this sophisticated lounge offers a panoramic vista that stretches as far as the eye can see. Step into a world of refinement and elegance as you savor handcrafted cocktails, premium spirits, and a curated wine list. Our culinary team presents a selection of gourmet delights that blend global influences with locally sourced ingredients. Whether you're seeking a romantic setting or a sophisticated gathering with friends, the Sky Lounge sets the stage for unforgettable moments and extraordinary experiences.
