Infinity Pool Bar

Welcome to the Infinity Pool Bar, where luxury and relaxation blend seamlessly with exquisite culinary delights and handcrafted beverages. Situated on the edge of an endless horizon, our bar offers a breathtaking setting to unwind and enjoy a delightful selection of treats.

Beverages:

1. Signature Poolside Punch: Quench your thirst with our tropical and refreshing concoction, a blend of fresh fruits and premium spirits that captures the essence of the exotic.
2. Mocktail Medley: For non-alcoholic options, we offer a range of refreshing mocktails bursting with vibrant flavors, perfect for a revitalizing poolside experience.
3. Craft Cocktails: Indulge in our expertly mixed craft cocktails, featuring innovative combinations of spirits, herbs, and fresh fruit infusions, promising a symphony of tastes.

Light Bites:

Poolside Sliders

Savor our mouthwatering sliders, featuring a choice of premium beef, tender chicken, or flavorful veggie patties, accompanied by a medley of gourmet toppings.

Fresh Ceviche

Immerse yourself in the flavors of the sea with our zesty ceviche, prepared with the catch of the day, marinated in lime juice, and enhanced with herbs and spices.

Crispy Calamari

Delight in tender calamari rings coated in a light and crispy batter, served with a tangy dipping sauce for the perfect poolside snack.

Mediterranean Bruschetta

Experience the essence of the Mediterranean with our classic bruschetta, featuring toasted artisanal bread topped with ripe tomatoes, basil, and balsamic glaze.

Salads:

1. Tropical Fruit Salad: Embrace the freshness of the tropics with a delightful mix of seasonal fruits and a hint of mint, a rejuvenating choice for a hot day by the pool.
2. Grilled Shrimp Salad: Treat yourself to a protein-packed salad featuring succulent grilled shrimp, mixed greens, and a zesty dressing that's light and satisfying.

Desserts:

1. Poolside Parfait: Layers of creamy Greek yogurt, seasonal fruits, and crunchy granola combine to create a guilt-free dessert that's perfect for poolside indulgence.
2. Chilled Chocolate Fondue: Dip into pure decadence with our chilled chocolate fondue served with an assortment of fresh fruits and marshmallows.
