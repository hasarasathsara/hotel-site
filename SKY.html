Sky Lounge

Welcome to the Sky Lounge, a haven of sophistication and refinement high above the city lights. Elevate your dining experience as you indulge in an exquisite array of culinary delights and handcrafted beverages, all while surrounded by breathtaking panoramic views.

Starters:

1.Lobster Bisque: Delight in the richness of our creamy lobster bisque, a velvety soup infused with the delicate flavors of the sea and garnished with a hint of fresh herbs.
2.Truffle Arancini: Experience Italian elegance with our golden-fried risotto balls filled with truffle-scented mushrooms, accompanied by a luscious dipping sauce.
3.Ahi Tuna Tartare: Embark on a journey of exquisite flavors with our finely diced ahi tuna, avocado, and sesame ginger dressing, served with crispy wonton chips.

Main Courses:

Prime Beef Fillet

Savor the succulence of our perfectly grilled prime beef fillet, complemented by a flavorful red wine reduction and a side of truffle mashed potatoes.

Pan-Seared Sea Bass

Immerse yourself in the taste of the ocean with our delicate sea bass fillet, seared to perfection and served with a lemon caper butter sauce.

Wild Mushroom Risotto

Experience the earthy indulgence of our creamy risotto infused with a medley of wild mushrooms, truffle oil, and Parmesan shavings.

Mediterranean Stuffed Bell Pepper

Delight in a vegetarian delight featuring roasted bell peppers stuffed with couscous, feta cheese, and a delightful blend of Mediterranean spices.

Desserts:

1. Decadent Chocolate Mousse: Satisfy your sweet cravings with our velvety chocolate mousse, topped with fresh berries and a sprinkle of cocoa powder.
2. Citrus Crème Brûlée: Experience a harmonious blend of creamy custard infused with a burst of citrus flavors, finished with a perfectly caramelized sugar crust.

Beverages:

1. Signature Skytini: Our exclusive cocktail creation, blending premium spirits, fresh fruit juices, and a touch of effervescence for a truly celestial experience.
2. Wine Selection: Indulge in our carefully curated wine list, featuring a selection of reds, whites, and rosés from renowned vineyards around the world.
3. Craft Beer Collection: For beer enthusiasts, we offer a handpicked assortment of craft beers, each chosen for its unique flavor profile and brewing artistry.
