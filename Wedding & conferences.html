Located amidst a romantic and exquisite beach location, Araliya Beach Resort and Spa offers you a host of immaculate wedding venues that even rivals the best wedding halls in Galle! With an exquisite 180 degrees view of the iconic beachfront, easily customisable fixtures and furniture, and well-curated catering service, we make your fairytale wedding dream a dazzling reality! The wedding venues are elaborate and spacious – the largest of it could comfortably accommodate up to 400 people. Also, the choice on offer is numerous – for which you could choose the right plan from a variety of wedding arrangements – the venues are pillar-less and they also feature a dedicated area for dining. Whether it is a private and intimate arrangement with a few of your friends and family or if it is a grand gala event that is embellished in grandeur we will indeed make your wedding day the most memorable day of your life!


For weddings, our team of dedicated professionals crafts an enchanting experience, ensuring every detail is thoughtfully curated to make your special day truly magical. From breathtaking beachfront ceremonies to elegant receptions, we create an ambiance that reflects your unique love story, leaving you with cherished memories that will last a lifetime.

Our state-of-the-art conference facilities provide the perfect backdrop for successful business events. Whether you're hosting a corporate meeting, team-building retreat, or international conference, our versatile venues, advanced technology, and personalized services guarantee a seamless and productive experience.

At Ovinray Beach Resort & Spa, we pride ourselves on delivering unparalleled service, extraordinary amenities, and a captivating coastal atmosphere. Embrace the tranquility of the ocean, the warmth of the sun, and the splendor of our resort as we turn your dreams into reality.
